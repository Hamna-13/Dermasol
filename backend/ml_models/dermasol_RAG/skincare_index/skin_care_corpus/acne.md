# Acne Skincare Guide

## What Is Acne?
Acne is a common skin condition that occurs when hair follicles become clogged with oil, dead skin cells, and bacteria. It can present as whiteheads, blackheads, pimples, pustules, and sometimes deeper nodules. Acne can be triggered by excess sebum production, hormonal changes, stress, or certain skincare products.

An effective acne routine focuses on:
- Reducing excess oil
- Helping skin shed dead cells
- Fighting acne-causing bacteria
- Supporting healing without over-drying

---

## Acne Skincare: Normal Skin

### Routine Guide
1. **Gentle Cleanser** – Cleans without stripping moisture.
2. **Salicylic Acid (BHA) Treatment** – Helps unclog pores.
3. **Benzoyl Peroxide Spot Treatment** – Targets bacteria.
4. **Light Moisturiser** – Prevents dryness and supports healing.
5. **Daily SPF** – Protects skin while using actives.

### Key Ingredients
- **Salicylic Acid** – Breaks up pore blockages and reduces blackheads.
- **Benzoyl Peroxide** – Reduces acne-causing bacteria.
- **Niacinamide** – Soothes irritation and supports barrier.

### Products
- Salicylic acid gel/toner
- Benzoyl peroxide treatment (2.5–5%)
- Lightweight moisturiser
- SPF 30+ broad-spectrum sunscreen

---

## Acne Skincare: Oily/Combination Skin

### Routine Guide
1. **Foaming Cleanser with Salicylic Acid** – Controls oil and exfoliates.
2. **Niacinamide Serum** – Helps reduce sebum and calm redness.
3. **Benzoyl Peroxide Treatment** – Apply to active breakouts.
4. **Oil-Free Moisturiser** – Hydrates without heaviness.
5. **SPF with Matte Finish** – Prevents shine while protecting skin.

### Key Ingredients
- **Salicylic Acid (BHA)** – Penetrates pores and exfoliates.
- **Niacinamide** – Balances oil production.
- **Clay Masks** – Occasional use to absorb excess oil.

### Products
- BHA cleanser or toner
- Niacinamide serum
- Benzoyl peroxide gel
- Oil-free moisturiser
- SPF with lightweight formula

---

## Acne Skincare: Dry/Sensitive Skin

### Routine Guide
1. **Hydrating Cleanser** – Cleans gently without irritation.
2. **Low-Concentration Salicylic Acid** – Use sparingly as a targeted treatment.
3. **Antimicrobial Spot Treatment** – Apply only where needed.
4. **Rich Moisturiser / Barrier Cream** – Prevents irritation and dryness.
5. **Daily SPF** – Protects skin and reduces irritation from actives.

### Key Ingredients
- **Salicylic Acid (low %) –** Helps exfoliate while minimising dryness.
- **Zinc** – Reduces inflammation.
- **Ceramides / Hyaluronic Acid** – Supports hydration.

### Products
- Gentle hydrating cleanser
- Targeted acne treatment
- Barrier-support moisturiser
- Broad-spectrum SPF 30+

---

## Core Acne-Fighting Ingredients

### Salicylic Acid
A beta hydroxy acid that dissolves pore blockages and exfoliates inside the follicle, reducing blackheads and reducing congestion.

### Benzoyl Peroxide
Targets acne-causing bacteria and reduces inflammation; effective as a spot treatment.

### Niacinamide
Multi-purpose ingredient that can help reduce excess oil production and calm irritation.

### Zinc / Sulfur
Support gentle antimicrobial action and help reduce inflammation, often in formulations for sensitive skin.

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- **Over-exfoliating** – Can worsen irritation.
- **Using heavy creams** on acne-prone skin – Can cause more breakouts.
- **Skipping sunscreen** – Sun exposure can darken acne marks and slow healing.
- **Mixing too many actives at once** – Find a simple, consistent routine.

---

## Routine Tips for Acne Success

✔ Use acne actives consistently but build up slowly.  
✔ Pair antimicrobial treatments with soothing ingredients to reduce irritation.  
✔ Always apply sunscreen to protect healing skin and prevent dark marks.  
✔ Track your progress; visible improvement may take 6–12 weeks.

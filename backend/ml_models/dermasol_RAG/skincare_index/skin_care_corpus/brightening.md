# Brightening Skincare Guide

## What Is Brightening?
Brightening refers to improving **dullness**, **uneven skin tone**, and **mild pigmentation** while enhancing skin **radiance and glow**. It does **not mean bleaching or changing natural skin colour** — instead it focuses on reducing dark spots, increasing luminosity, and creating an even, healthy-looking complexion suited for all skin types.

Brightening routines often combine:
- **Antioxidants** to protect against free radicals
- **Exfoliants** to remove dead skin cells
- **UV protection** to prevent further pigmentation  
Regular sunscreen use is essential to maintain brightening results and prevent darkening caused by sun exposure.

---

## Brightening Skincare: Normal Skin

### Routine Guide
1. **Gentle Cleanser** – Removes dirt and impurities without stripping moisture.
2. **Antioxidant Serum (Vitamin C)** – Helps protect skin and reduce dullness.
3. **Hydrating Moisturiser** – Keeps skin balanced and plump.
4. **Daily Broad-Spectrum SPF** – Protects against UV damage that contributes to dullness and spots.

### Key Ingredients
- **Vitamin C** – Potent antioxidant that helps even skin tone and fade mild discoloration.
- **Niacinamide** – Improves radiance and brightens while soothing.
- **Tranexamic Acid** – Helps correct dark patches and balance skin tone.

### Products
- Vitamin C serum (mid-strength formulas)
- Niacinamide 5%
- SPF 30+ broad-spectrum sunscreen

---

## Brightening Skincare: Oily/Combination Skin

### Routine Guide
1. **Foaming Cleanser** – Removes excess oil and impurities.
2. **L-Ascorbic Acid Serum (Vitamin C)** – Lightweight antioxidant for glow and tone.
3. **Exfoliating Toner (AHA/BHA)** – Gentle acid exfoliation improves texture and reveals brighter skin.
4. **Oil-Free Moisturiser** – Hydrates without heaviness.
5. **SPF Every Day** – Essential to protect new cells and prevent pigmentation.

### Key Ingredients
- **Vitamin C (L-ascorbic acid)** – Brightens and improves tone with antioxidant protection.
- **Niacinamide** – Controls shine and balances skin tone.
- **Glycolic Acid (AHA)** – Mild exfoliant that helps slough off dead skin cells for a fresh look.

### Products
- Vitamin C serum (lightweight gel)
- Glycolic or lactic acid exfoliant (1–5%)
- Oil-free hyaluronic acid moisturiser
- Broad-spectrum SPF 50+

---

## Brightening Skincare: Dry/Sensitive Skin

### Routine Guide
1. **Creamy Hydrating Cleanser** – Cleans gently without drying out.
2. **Antioxidant Serum (Gentle Vitamin C or Niacinamide)** – Enhances glow with less irritation.
3. **Hydrating Serum (Hyaluronic Acid)** – Provides deep moisture.
4. **Rich Moisturiser or Barrier Cream** – Seals in hydration and improves texture.
5. **SPF Daily** – Protects from UV-induced dullness.

### Key Ingredients
- **Niacinamide** – Well-tolerated brightening ingredient for sensitive skin.
- **Alpha Arbutin** – Gentle tyrosinase inhibiting brightener with low irritation.
- **Rice Extract** – Traditional natural brightener and skin soother.

### Products
- Gentle Vitamin C or niacinamide serum
- Hyaluronic acid serum
- Rich moisturiser (ceramide/honey/rice extract)
- Daily SPF 30+

---

## Core Brightening Ingredients

### Vitamin C
A powerful antioxidant that combats dullness and supports collagen, helping fade mild dark spots. Best used in the morning before sunscreen.

### Niacinamide
Supports an even tone, reduces visibility of dullness, and improves texture without irritation.

### Tranexamic Acid
Targets stubborn pigmentation and uneven patches, beneficial in serums for brightening.

### Alpha Arbutin / Kojic Acid
Tyrosinase-inhibiting agents that help fade dark spots and even tone (use at low concentrations).

---

## Sunscreen: Non-Negotiable
Sun exposure is one of the *major causes* of pigmentation and dullness. Daily use of SPF 30+ protects your skin and preserves brightening progress.

---

## Routine Tips for Best Results

✔ Be **consistent** — brightening takes 8–12 weeks for visible changes.
✔ Introduce active ingredients gradually to avoid irritation.
✔ Pair antioxidants with sunscreen every day.
